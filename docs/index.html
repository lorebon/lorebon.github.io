Lorenzo Bonasera

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at DTU Management, working on the DECIDE project with David Pisinger. I obtained my PhD in operations research from the University of Pavia and the University of Lugano (USI), under the supervision of Stefano Gualandi. I previously worked as a research scientist at the German Aerospace Center (DLR) in the Institute for AI Safety and Security, applying Generative AI methods to enhance situational awareness in the maritime domain.

My main research agenda focuses on reducing the arbitrariness in data-driven decision making. I pursue this goal through two complementary research directions:

  • developing interpretable machine learning models;
  • designing decision-support systems that account for predictive and prescriptive multiplicity.
